About Us

Extreme Workstation of Computers was founded by Mr Sachin Gulati with the mission to provide top-quality computer services to our community. We understand how important technology is in today’s world, whether it's for personal use, education, or business.

Beyond repairs, we proudly offer a fully equipped cyber cafe space where customers can access fast internet and printing services in a safe and comfortable environment. Our goal is to make technology accessible and convenient for everyone.

Recognizing the growing need for digital literacy, we also provide comprehensive computer courses tailored to beginners and intermediate learners. Our curriculum covers essential skills such as Microsoft Office applications, internet navigation, Tally accounting software, and more. We believe in empowering our students with practical knowledge that can open new doors in education and career opportunities.

Additionally, we assist our clients with various types of form fillings — including school and college admission forms, job applications, and Provident Fund-related documentation. Our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you step-by-step, ensuring accuracy and timely submissions.

At Extreme Workstation of Computers , customer satisfaction is our top priority. We combine technical expertise, personalized service, and affordable pricing to meet your needs. Whether you’re facing a tech emergency, want to learn new skills, or need help with paperwork, we’re your trusted partner.

We continuously update our services and courses to keep pace with evolving technology trends, so you always get the latest and best. Thank you for choosing Extreme Workstation of Computers — your one-stop solution for all things tech.
